# Google Reviews — Request Toolkit

Reviews are the single strongest lever for local ranking and conversion.
You have a 5.0★ rating with 4 reviews; a realistic near-term goal is **20+**.
Getting there is simple: ask every happy client, make it one tap, and follow up once.

## 1. Get your one-tap review link

The templates below use a placeholder link. Replace it with your real one-tap link:

1. Open your **Google Business Profile** (business.google.com) while signed in as the owner.
2. On the dashboard, click **"Ask for reviews"** (or **Get more reviews**).
3. Google gives you a short link that looks like `https://g.page/r/XXXXXXXX/review`.
4. Copy that link and use it everywhere below.

Once you send it to me, I'll bake it into the on-site **/review/** page and the QR code
so everything points to the true one-tap destination.

## 3. Best practices

- **Ask at the peak** — right after closing or a clear win, not weeks later.
- **Personalize the first line** — use their name and one specific detail.
- **One tap only** — always link straight to the review form; never ask them to search.
- **Follow up once** — a single gentle reminder after ~5 days is fine; no more.
- **Respond to every review** — a brief, warm reply (even to 5★) signals attentiveness and helps ranking.
- **Never incentivize** — no discounts, gifts, or credits for reviews. It's against Google policy and can get reviews removed.
- **Print the QR** — add `review/qr.png` to business cards, closing folders, and email signatures.
