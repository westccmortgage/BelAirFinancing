Holmby Hills

Legacy estates, exceptional loan sizes

Together with Bel Air and Beverly Hills, Holmby Hills completes the Platinum Triangle. Even in that company it stands apart: a compact neighborhood of grand estates, many held by the same families for decades, trading only rarely and at the very top of the market.

Financing at this level is seldom about a single number. Loan balances are large, appraisals rest on a thin set of comparables, and ownership is often layered through trusts and entities. Refinances of long-held homes bring their own considerations around equity, liquidity, and planning.

Every element — from asset positioning to timing — deserves to be mapped before a transaction begins.

  • Very large loan balances
  • Appraisals with limited comparable sales
  • Trust, estate, and entity ownership
  • Cash-out and equity strategy on long-held homes
  • Asset-based and portfolio review
  • Discretion at the top of the market
