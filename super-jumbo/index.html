Overview

What super-jumbo financing is

Super-jumbo generally refers to loan amounts well beyond standard jumbo thresholds. At this level, financing is typically handled through select portfolio and Non-QM lending channels rather than conventional programs — each with its own documentation, reserve, and appraisal expectations.

For qualified borrowers, loan amounts up to $10 million may be available. The right path depends on how income and assets are structured, the property itself, and the timeline — which is why every scenario begins with a review, not a quote.

  • Are purchasing or refinancing above standard jumbo limits
  • Have complex or entity-held income
  • Qualify on assets or bank-statement cash flow
  • Prefer interest-only or portfolio structures
  • Are financing unique or high-value properties
  • Value discretion and careful timing
