Beverly Hills

One city, several very different markets

Few places pack as much variety into as little geography as Beverly Hills. The Flats offer prized flat-lot homes on tree-lined streets; the hills hold gated estates with views; and the Wilshire Corridor is lined with luxury high-rises that trade like nowhere else in the region.

That range shapes financing. Condominium purchases require project-level review that many lenders handle poorly. Estate purchases push into jumbo territory with appraisal complexity. And a large share of buyers are founders, entertainment-industry professionals, and international purchasers whose income and assets need careful presentation.

The right structure depends on which Beverly Hills you are buying into — and on the shape of your own balance sheet.

  • Condominium and high-rise project review
  • Jumbo loan amounts across property types
  • Self-employed and equity-based income
  • Asset and portfolio positioning
  • Entity and cross-border considerations
  • A wide spread of property values
